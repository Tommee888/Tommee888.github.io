Paul the Alien

There once was a nice alien named Paul.
Paul is a very special alien.
Paul's abilities consists of:

  • healing
  • invisibility
  • knowledge transfer
  • resurrection

Paul has the power to take over the world, and he chooses you as a partner to help him decide if he should be good, or evil.
He looks at you with his big eyes and tells you, "Do whats best for this world bro."

You make him evil.

You make him good.
